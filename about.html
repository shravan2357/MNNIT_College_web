About MNNIT Allahabad

A Legacy of Excellence in Technical Education and Research

Our Legacy

Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad (MNNIT) was established in 1961 as a premier technical institution in India. Recognized as an Institute of National Importance, MNNIT continues to produce top engineers, researchers, and innovators.

Our Mission

To advance knowledge and educate students in science, technology, and other areas of scholarship, fostering innovation and leadership.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence that provides world-class education and contributes to societal development through research and innovation.
